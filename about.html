About ClayMeArt

Welcome to the world of ClayMeArt. Here you will find how a lump of clay becomes a work of art. You are invited to explore this new and fascinating world of clay craft. So buckle up, and enjoy the ride.

This is a wonderful and colorful world of Flower-Land where everything is made of DecoClay, an incredibly light air-dried material that was originated in Japan more than two decades ago. The air in Flower-Land is always clean and fresh thanks to DecoClay's non-toxicity and eco-frendliness. Every flower that blooms on these lands is one-of-a-kind masterpiece, handcrafted piece by piece and petal by petal with care and love by creative people who live in Flower-Land. In addition, due to DecoClay's surprising strength and durability all our creations will last for many years to come.

We hope you enjoyed your journey and learned a bit about the world of floral clay art. It is an amazing and relatively new concept in floral design that more and more people are falling in love with every day. Come again!

About Me

Unlike most crafters out there, I was not initially interested in crafting or DIYing. I was never creative or artistic, at least not with my hands. The genesis for my creativity was time; time to let my imagination wander and think how I could use the hours and days to create something new, something beautiful and extraordinary.

My first creations started out with various clay and salt dough models and were not satisfactory from an artistic slant. It was when I came upon DecoClay that I was able to let the creative juices flow, and it thus gave me the ability to create the many variations of flower bouquets and arrangements which now adorn my portfolio. The rest as they say is history. What was a hobby has become a dream. What was once a dream has now blossomed into ClayMeArt.
