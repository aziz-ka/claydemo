About ClayMeArt

Welcome to ClayMeArt, where a lump of clay becomes a work of art. You are invited to explore a new and fascinating world of clay craft.

This is a wonderful and colorful Flower-Land where everything is made of DecoClay, an incredibly light air-dried material that was originated in Japan more than two decades ago. The air in Flower-Land is always clean and fresh thanks to DecoClay's non-toxicity and eco-friendliness. Every flower that blooms on these lands is one of a kind masterpiece, handcrafted piece by piece and petal by petal with care and love and turned into stunning bouquets, arrangements and accessories. Last but not least, DecoClay's surprising strength and durability allows all our creations to last for many years to come.

We hope you learned a bit about the world of floral clay art. It is an amazing and relatively new concept in floral design that more and more people are falling in love with every day. Come back again!

About Artist

It all began from the moment my husband took me to a local craft store for the first time. Where I was born and raised there weren't any specialized stores for DIYers and artists. Thus, I was never really into crafting or DIYing while growing up. Walking in I was stunned by the variety of products and felt as if I was a kid in the candy store wanting to try everything. For some reason the very first thing I got my hands on was polymer clay. Since that day, clay sculpting has been part of my life and I enjoy evry bit of it.

My first creations started out with various clay and salt dough models. They were not perfect, but it kindled a spark. It was when I came across DecoClay that I began crafting clay flowers which now adorn my portfolio. The rest, as they say, is history.
